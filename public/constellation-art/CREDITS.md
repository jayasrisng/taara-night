# Constellation artwork credits

The 85 grayscale constellation illustrations in this directory come from
Stellarium's **Modern** sky culture and were drawn by Johan Meuris. They are
distributed under the [Free Art License 1.3](https://artlibre.org/licence/lal/en/).

- Source: https://github.com/Stellarium/stellarium/tree/master/skycultures/modern/illustrations
- Artist: Johan Meuris — https://johanmeuris.eu/work/stellarium-constellation-art/
- Modifications in TaaraNight: resized, packed into an atlas, tinted, glowed,
  and screen-blended at runtime. Argo Navis is reused for its modern component
  constellations Carina, Puppis, and Vela.

`serpens-generated.png`, `taurus-cinematic.png`, and `taurus-cinematic-v2.png` are original generated
assets created for TaaraNight by Jayasri Guthula with OpenAI image generation.
They are composited over real catalogue stars and connecting figures at runtime.
The v2 Taurus sprite follows the pose and anchor composition of Johan Meuris'
credited Stellarium Taurus plate so its real catalogue stars register cleanly.

The real star positions and constellation figures remain sourced and credited
separately in the project README.
